Helping Auto Rickshaw Driver

#Switch ToElectric

Auto rickshaw drivers play a crucial role in urban transport, providing affordable mobility.Many drivers struggle with fluctuating incomes due to competition and rising fuel costs. Their workdays are long, often exceeding 12 hours, to earn a decent livelihood.Exposure to pollution and long hours can lead to health problems, making access to healthcare vital. Many drivers support their families.

Our Aim?

Helping auto-rickshaw drivers thrive while making the planet greener. They are all about using smart technology like IoT to make auto-rickshaws better. and other eco-friendly rides.

Our Coversion

conversion process involves removing the existing engine and fuel system, and replacing them with a powerful electric motor and battery pack

Benifits of Conversion

No Fuel Costs with the continued rise in fuel prices, owning an electric car offers significant savings. Electric vehicles eliminate the need for regular refueling with petrol or diesel.

Introduction To Our Electric Auto

Electric auto rickshaws are eco-friendly urban transport options, reducing emissions and noise.
They offer cost-effective commuting,ease of navigation in congested areas, and contribute to sustainable city development
and mobility.
