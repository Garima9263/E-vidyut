Diagnostix

An easy to set-up, non-invasive data acquisition tool for independent analysis of vehicle performance and telemetric data. Useful for cost-effective vehicle design and compliance preparation. Pairs with any android device with Bluetooth 2.1 or greater and works seamlessly with the bundled app.

Use Cases:

Diagnostix empowers you to become your own vehicle detective. By offering real-time telemetry data, it gives you a comprehensive overview of your vehicle's health. You can proactively identify and address potential faults before they become bigger problems. Planning trips becomes a breeze with Diagnostix's accurate range estimation and optimization tools, and exploring new routes is aided by its terrain and track mapping functionalities.

Specifications:
Diagnostix packs a powerful punch in a compact design. With 3 channels for precise current and voltage measurement, it handles a wide range of vehicles thanks to voltage sensing up to 600V DC and 1100V AC, and non-contact current sense up to 200A. Battery care is a priority with its state-of-the-art algorithm for Battery State of Charge (SoC) monitoring. But Diagnostix goes beyond just electrical data. It incorporates a 3-axis gyroscope and accelerometer for motion and orientation tracking, along with 2 temperature sensors for thermal monitoring. For even greater versatility, it offers 4 digital input channels for RPM and various sensor applications, and 6 digital input channels for on/off detection. Data output options include both wired Serial Port (UART) and convenient real-time Bluetooth streaming to the dedicated Android app.

CAN Based Graphical Instrument Cluster

Specifications:

CAN Based Graphical Instrument Cluster offers a customizable and data-rich experience for vehicles. The 3.4" sunlight-readable LCD display connects seamlessly with CAN 2.0b networks, providing odometer, trip meter, and timekeeping functions. Rugged IP67 enclosures can be tailored to specific needs, and the display adheres to AIS-071 visibility standards. Users can even benefit from vehicle range estimation with a customizable algorithm. An optional Bluetooth system allows for data logging and smartphone connectivity, while the cluster boasts 6 indicator lights, an analog speed input, and a CAN bus input for versatile data integration.
